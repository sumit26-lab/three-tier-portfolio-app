# Portfolio Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Drawing inspiration from modern portfolio sites like those of successful academics and professionals (Behance, Dribbble portfolios), focusing on clean presentation of achievements and professional credibility.

## Core Design Elements

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: 220 25% 15% (Deep navy for headers and primary text)
- **Secondary**: 220 15% 45% (Mid-tone gray for secondary text)
- **Accent**: 220 85% 55% (Professional blue for CTAs and highlights)
- **Background**: 0 0% 98% (Off-white) / Dark mode: 220 20% 8%
- **Surface**: 0 0% 100% (Pure white cards) / Dark mode: 220 15% 12%

### Typography
- **Primary**: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, professional readability
- **Accent**: Playfair Display (Google Fonts) - elegant headers for name/titles
- **Hierarchy**: h1 (48px), h2 (36px), h3 (24px), body (16px), small (14px)

### Layout System
**Tailwind Spacing**: Consistent use of 4, 8, 12, 16, 24 units (p-4, m-8, gap-12, etc.)
- Container max-width: 6xl with centered content
- Grid system: 12-column for desktop, stacked for mobile
- Section padding: py-16 for major sections

### Component Library
- **Navigation**: Fixed header with smooth scroll navigation
- **Cards**: Elevated white cards with subtle shadows for experience/education
- **Timeline**: Vertical timeline for education and career progression
- **Buttons**: Primary (filled blue), Secondary (outline), Text links
- **Typography**: Consistent heading hierarchy with proper line spacing

## Specific Sections

### Hero Section
- **Layout**: Split layout - left text, right professional headshot placeholder
- **Content**: Name in Playfair Display, title/role, brief tagline, CTA buttons
- **Background**: Clean gradient from white to very light blue (220 60% 98%)

### Professional Sections
- **Experience**: Timeline cards with role, company, dates, achievements
- **Education**: Academic timeline with institutions, degrees, years
- **Skills**: Organized grid with categories (Technical, Languages, Certifications)
- **Accomplishments**: Badge-style cards for sports achievements and recognitions

### Contact Section
- **Layout**: Centered with professional contact form and details
- **Background**: Subtle gradient background (220 40% 96%)

## Images
- **Hero Image**: Professional headshot (300x300px, circular crop)
- **Section Icons**: Use Heroicons for section headers and skill categories
- **Achievement Badges**: Small icons for sports/academic accomplishments
- **No large hero background image** - keep focus on content and professionalism

## Key Principles
- **Professional Credibility**: Clean, academic-inspired design
- **Readability**: High contrast, generous whitespace, clear hierarchy
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first approach with tablet/desktop enhancements
- **Performance**: Minimal animations, fast loading, optimized images
- **Accessibility**: Proper heading structure, keyboard navigation, WCAG compliance

This design emphasizes professionalism and academic achievement while maintaining modern web standards and excellent user experience across all devices.
